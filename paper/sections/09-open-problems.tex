\subsection{Principled Forgetting}

\todo{Most systems have no forgetting or single-curve decay. Bjork
dual-strength forgetting is implemented by one system (Alaya). The
field needs: empirical comparison of forgetting models on agent
benchmarks, forgetting as a retrieval quality mechanism (not just
storage management), principled forgetting that improves downstream
task performance.}

\subsection{Preference Emergence vs. Extraction}

\todo{The tradeoff: LLM extraction (fast, explicit, brittle) vs.
emergent crystallization (slow, implicit, robust). No benchmark
compares these approaches. Research needed on: when do preferences
need explicit extraction vs. organic emergence?}
